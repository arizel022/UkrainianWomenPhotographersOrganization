100 women

The first group project in which we represent an idea to gather one hundred women photographers from all over Ukraine in order to visualize what it means to be a woman and your own muse. Is there something more behind a regular selfie? This project reflects the popular interest in selfies as a successful self representation, and also highlights the popular phenomenon as female art. 100 women submitted images and words about what it means to be your own muse. Can there be deeper meaning behind our selfies? The goal of the project is to unite women photographers from different cities of Ukraine, as well as to analyze the so-called female art.
