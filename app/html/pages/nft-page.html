  • Female Nature

    This is a collection of selected works of Ukrainian female photographers on the Foundation platform. In our lens - women through the eyes of women themselves. At the heart of the collection is a conceptual polylogue about the nature of a woman, the diversity of her nature and the absolute impossibility of getting to know her completely. Her photo-fixation in the moment does not make female nature a more understandable and easily perceptible thought, but moves it into the category of a conceptually and aesthetically perceptible idea.
