Diary. Private & Public

The Diaries project is our message encrypted in pictures about women feelings during the period that has started on February 24.2022. It can be easy and clear without words, it can be difficult and expressed by excessive explanation, short or prolonged, dramatic or funny, shocking or cute. But it always will be personal. Most of the diaries are an attempt to cope stress, depression and hard circumstances. In this project, personal interpretation of the circumstances becomes important, there is no objectivity here, it is deeply personal, despite being tied to events and dates. Transform individual experience into a concentrated image, an empathic response in perception and made such an effect of feeling a reflection which transformed into an individual work of art are revealed.
